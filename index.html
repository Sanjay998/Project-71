what's new

  • New Playground

    Peter Hansen elementary school has added a new playground. The previous playgrounds were really good, but the problem was that people were getting bored and some people play really rough on the play structure which causes it to break a part of it and it takes a long time to fix it. The teachers thought it would be better to add a new, more stronger and fun playground. After a while, it was completed and everything worked out.

  • New Science Building

    Peter Hansen elementary school has added a new science building. We usually use the gym to present or do science fairs or science olympiads. Some classes would be cancelled due to these events. The principals realized that we shouldn't just cancell classes, so they made a science building. Now that we have this there will be no skipping classes.
